About our (fictional) client Studentpartiet

Studentpartiet is a new Norwegian political party, fighting for students' interests. The party was founded this year, by students getting tired of not getting their voices heard. The party is a pro-academia and has students as their main target demographics, but is open for everyone with pro-academic political views. The party is positioning itself for the next election and need a website to make their political views easily available to the public.

Our (fictional) contact person: Ragnhild Kristensen

Ragnhild Kristensen is studying public communication at NTNU, and is one of the founder of this party, concerned over the underrepresentation of young people as elected officials. She is the chief of communication in Studentpartiet. She contacted us because the party needed a web site. As a public communication student, she has much knowledge of how to communicate to the public much efficiently. Therefore, she has made several specific requirements for the website. We will have communication with her throughout the project.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to spread information about Studentpartiet and it's political views, in order to get public support. It is also to make the audience more familiar with Studentpartiet's politicians. In other words, this page is primarily for people who might consider to support the party, and not the party's supporters themselves, however, the site is also for making supporters able to donate to the party and making them able to join the party. The voter participation rate is much lower for young adults than the overall population. This site is also for trying to make young people engage in politics in general, as that would mean a lager focus on student's interests.

Audience

Studentpartiet's primary audience is students, who are usually quite young (often between 18 and 30) and experienced at using the web. Therefore having a modern and appealing appearance is prioritized over accessibility. Also young people surfing on the Internet, often use a short period of time on each web site, therefore it is important to make the party's main political objective promoted on the front page, so that people who are just having a short visit get an intuition of the party's political views. Young people also usually use updated browsers, something that makes CSS-compability issues quite unlikely, but it is important for Studentpartiet that it is a party for everyone, and not just young and modern people, as many in the public would probably support Studentpartiet's pro-academic views, even if they are not a part of academia themselves. Social media is quite important for young people, this site's main demographic, therefore it is important that the site links to Stundentpartiet's accounts on social media, and ask visitors to follow them. It has proven harder to get young people to the polls than the population overall, which makes it important that this site also promote activism, and not just educate the audience about Studentpartiet's views.

Requirements

  1. You should be able to compare Studentpartiet's views to those of other political parties.
  2. Studentpartiet's main political objectives are clearly shown on the front page.
  3. The background shouldn't be too blunt, as that may steal the visitors focus from the political views.
  4. The website should be easily navigated with a practical menu in the header.
  5. The site design should be modern in order to appeal to adolescents.
  6. However the design should also be understandable to older users, as Studentpartiet tries to position itself as a party for everyone.
  7. The site design is should be based upon an orange color, because it is Studentpartiet's political color.
  8. The site should promote photos of happy people, as that may lead to the audience mentally connect the party with positive emotions .
  9. The user should be able to find ways to contact the party if there are any questions.

The web site should consist of:

  1. Front page

    The page should contain a summary of the party's main political objectives and a photo of smiling humans.

    The summary of the main political objective is supposed to give visitors an intuition of what the party's political stances.

    The photos of smiling humans are supposed to give the visitors positive connections to the party and it's politics

  2. Contact page

    The page should contain an address, a phone number, and an email address.

    The page should also contain links Studentpartiet's accounts on different social media, like Facebook and Twitter.

    The purpose of the page is to make it easy for visitors to contact the party if they have any questions about the party and it's political views. The point of the social media links is so that vistors may follow the party, for the latest news about them. Furthermore social media is the prefered communication channel for the youths, the site's main demographic.

  3. Political program

    The page should contain Studentpartiet's political views and a way to compare those views to that of other political parties.

    The purpose of this page is to make the party's political views easily accessible if people wonder what the party's views are in one certain area, and how they compare to those of other parties. This is only for people wondering what their views is in a certain area, the party's main political objectives are promoted on the front page.

  4. Our politicians

    The page should contain pictures and a short summary of Studentpartiet's politicians, so that the visitors can learn to trust them, and eventually vote for them.

    People usually trust people if they feel they know them on a deeper level, therefore this page intend to tell the personal stories of Stundentpartiet's politicians.

    The page should also contain links to the politicians' accounts on social media, both so that people can get better acquainted with the party's politicians and so that people can get the latest news from their politicians and ask them questions if they have any.

  5. About page

    The page should contain some basic information about the party, including it's history, shown on a timeline.

    As Studentpartiet is a brand new and mostly unknown party, this page is important, as the public would like some basic information about the party to understand it's history and it's goals.

    Another purpose of this page is to make it clear to people that this is a serious party, when people hasn't heard of it before.

  6. Supporting page

    The page should contain a way to donate to the party and a way to become a member of the party.

    The page should also include dates for political meetings and demonstration, so that supporters find a way to promote the party's views, and people interested in the party find a meeting so that they can discuss the party's politics.

    The page should also include open positions in the party, and ways to volunteer for the party. Among other things, they need someone volunteering as responsible for the web page, when this project is done

    The purpose of this site is for supporters of the party to find way to help the party. It is also to promote activism among students, by helping them find ways to get their voices heard.

The web site after the project:

After the project it is Studentpartiets responsible for hosting and updating the site. The party will have someone experinced at web technology as volunteer as the one responsible for the webpage, who will maintain the site.
