About our client Studentpartiet

Studentpartiet is a new Norwegian political party, fighting for students' interests. The party was founded this year, by students getting tiered of not getting their voices heard. The party is positioning itself for the next election and need a website to make their political views easily available to the public.

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to spread information about Studentpartiet's political views, in order to convince the public. It is also to make the audience more familiar with Studentpartiet's politicians. The site is also for making supporters able to donate to them and making them able to join the party.

Audience

Studentpartiet's primary audience is students, who are usually quite experinced at using the web. Therefore having a modern and appealing appearance is prioritized over accessibility.

Requirements

  1. You should be able to compare Studentpartiet's views to those of other political parties.
  2. Studentpartiet's main political objectives are clearly shown on the front page.
  3. The background shouldn't be too blunt, as that may steal the visitors focus from the political views.
  4. The website should be easily naviated with a practical meny in the header.
  5. The site design should be modern in order to appeal to adolescents.
  6. The site design is should be based upon an orange color, because it is Studentpartiet's political color.
  7. The site should promote photos of happy people, as that may lead to the audience mentally connect the party with positive emotions .
  8. The user should be able to find ways to contact the party if there is any questions.

The web site should consist of:

  1. Front page

    The page should contain a summary of the party's main political objectives and a photo of smiling humans.

  2. Contact page

    The page should contain an address, a phone number, and an email address

  3. Political program

    The page should contain Studentpartiet's political views and a way to compare those views to that of other political parties.

  4. Our politicians

    The page should contain pictures and a short summary of Studentpartiet's politicians, so that the visitors can learn to trust them, and eventually vote for them.

  5. About page

    The page should contain some basic information about the party, including it's history, shown on a timeline.

  6. Supporting page

    The page should contain a way to donate to the party and a way to become a member of the party.
