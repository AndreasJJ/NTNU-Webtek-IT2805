About our (fictional) client Studentpartiet

  • What:

    Studentpartiet is a new Norwegian political party, fighting for students' interests.

  • When:

    The party was founded this year.

  • Why:

    Students getting tired of not having their voices heard.

  • Who:

    The party is pro-academia and has students as their main target demographics, but is open for everyone with pro-academic political views.

  • Why a website:

    The party is positioning itself for the next election and needs a website to make their political views easily available to the public.


Our (fictional) contact person: Ragnhild Kristensen

  • Role at Studentpartiet:

    She is the chief of communication at Studentpartiet, and one of its founders.

  • Role in this project:

    As a public communication student, she has much knowledge of how to communicate to the public efficiently. Therefore, she has made several specific requirements for the website.

    We will communicate with her throughout the project.


Purpose

  • To spread information about Studentpartiet and its political views, in order to get public support.

  • Make the visitors more familiar with Studentpartiet's politicians.

  • Making supporters able to donate to the party and making them able to join the party.

  • Make young people engage in politics in general, as that would mean a larger focus on student's interests.

Audience

  • Who are they:

    Studentpartiet's primary audience is students, who are usually quite young (often between 18 and 30) and experienced at using the web.

  • What are the consequences:

    1. Having a modern and appealing appearance is prioritized over accessibility.
    2. Young people surfing on the Internet often use a short period of time on each website, therefore it is important to make the party's main political objective promoted on the front page, so that people who are just having a short visit get an intuition of the party's political views.
    3. Young people also usually use updated browsers, something that makes CSS-compability issues quite unlikely.
    4. Social media is quite important for young people, this site's main demographic, therefore it is important that the site links to Stundentpartiet's accounts on social media, and asks visitors to follow them.
    5. It has proven harder to get young people to the polls than the population overall, which makes it important that this site also promotes activism, and not just educate the audience about Studentpartiet's views.

Functional requirements

  1. You should be able to compare Studentpartiet's views to those of other political parties when you select political party and political area.
  2. Studentpartiet's main political objectives should be shown on the frontpage.
  3. The website should have a practical menu in the header, that is, linking to the most used sites (front page, political program, supporting page and the contact page).
  4. The site design is based upon an orange color, because it is Studentpartiet's political color.
  5. The site should use photos of happy people (that may lead to the audience mentally connecting the party with positivity).
  6. The contact information should be easily found, by having contact information in the footer and link to the contact page in the navigation bar.
  7. The site should have a way to join and to donate to the party.

Non-functional requirements

  1. The background shouldn't be too blunt, as that may steal the visitors focus from the political content.
  2. The site design should be modern in order to appeal to adolescents.

The web site should consist of:

  1. Front page

    • Content:

      The page should contain a summary of the party's main political objectives and a photo of smiling humans.

    • Purpose:

      The summary of the main political objective is supposed to give visitors an intuition of what the party's political stances are.

      The photos of smiling humans are supposed to give the visitors positive connections to the party and its politics.

  2. Contact page

    • Content:

      The page should contain an address, a phone number, and an email address.

      The page should also contain links of Studentpartiet's accounts on different social media, like Facebook and Twitter.

    • Purpose:

      The purpose of the page is to make it easy for visitors to contact the party if they have any questions about the party and it's political views.

      The point of the social media links is so that vistors may follow the party, for the latest news about them.

      Furthermore social media is the prefered communication channel for the youths, the site's main demographic.

  3. Political program

    • Content:

      The page should contain Studentpartiet's political views and a way to compare those views to that of other political parties.

    • Purpose:

      The purpose of this page is to make the party's political views easily accessible if people wonder what the party's views are in one certain area, and how they compare to those of other parties.

      This is only for people wondering what their views is in a certain area, the party's main political objectives are promoted on the front page.

  4. Our politicians

    • Content:

      The page should contain pictures and a short summary of Studentpartiet's politicians, so that the visitors can learn to trust them, and eventually vote for them.

      The page should also contain links to the politicians' accounts on social media, both so that people can get better acquainted with the party's politicians and so that people can get the latest news from their politicians and ask them questions if they have any.

    • Purpose:

      People usually trust people if they feel they know them on a deeper level, therefore this page intends to tell the personal stories of Stundentpartiet's politicians.

  5. About page

    • Content:

      The page should contain some basic information about the party, including it's history, shown on a timeline.

    • Purpose:

      As Studentpartiet is a brand new and mostly unknown party, this page is important, as the public would like some basic information about the party to understand it's history and goals.

      Another purpose of this page is to make it clear to people that this is a serious party, if they haven't heard of it before.

  6. Supporting page

    • Content:

      The page should contain a way to donate to the party and a way to become a member of the party.

      The page should also include dates for political meetings and demonstrations.

      The page should also include open positions in the party, and ways to volunteer for the party.

    • Purpose:

      The purpose of this site is for supporters of the party to find way to help the party. It is also to promote activism among students, by helping them find ways to get their voices heard.

  7. Site map

    • Content:

      The page should contain a list of all the pages on this site. They will be sorted by a hierarchy of which pages link to others.

      The page would simply be an unordered list with unordered lists inside each other.

    • Purpose:

      The purpose of this page is so that visitors can find the page they are looking for, by having an easily read list.

The web site after the project:

After the project it is Studentpartiets responsibility to maintain the site. The party will have someone experinced at web technology being responsible for the webpage.
